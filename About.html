Background of the Firm

Elena Consultants was founded in 2013 and is located in Bungoma town, Red Hat Building, opposite Bungoma Post Office Building. The core business of the firm is spatial planning and environmental impact assessment and audits. The firm offers services to the entire of western region. Services offered include; giving expert advice on optimal, effective and efficient use of Land. This includes preparation of change of user reports, subdivision schemes, layout plans for various institutions, Ideal locations for various land uses, situational analysis for planning purposes in pursuant with the Physical Planning Act, cap 286 of the laws of Kenya, Urban areas and cities act, 2012.

Elena Consultants is a local Kenyan private firm registered on 06th April 2017 under registration certificate number PVT-AAABDWO in accordance with the provisions of the Registration o f companies Act, 2015 and rule made there under.

The firm has professionals working for it and associates who are all highly experienced and have a successful track record in consultancy services as evidenced by the attached curriculum vitaes.

The firm is therefore strategically placed to provide services to its wide range of clients, being national government, county government, various institutions and individual developers. The company boasts of professional staff with over fourteen years of work experience within the above area of focus. It is fully registered under the Companies Act (CAP 486, repealed in 2015) and has acquired other statutory requirements, namely VAT and PIN Certificates and TAX compliance. At the same time, the Principal Consultants of the Company are also duly registered by the relevant Government regulatory bodies to engage in the services that the Company offers. These regulatory bodies include the Physical Planners Registration Board, the National Environment Management Authority, the Kenya Institute of Planners, and the Environment Institute of Kenya.
